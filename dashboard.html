📊 Business Analyst Briefing
6 findings ranked by urgency — what to act on and in what order
🔴 Critical — Fix Within 48h
Every lead submitted so far has been lost
The form fires console.log and stops. No database, no Klaviyo, no Google Sheet. You've been collecting intent from real Mumbai dog owners and discarding it. These people will never be re-engaged — the window closes when they leave Earth Cafe.
→ Wire Klaviyo subscribe + a Sheets/Airtable webhook as a redundant backup. 2h of work, permanent fix.
🔴 Critical — Fix This Week
No analytics = no way to optimise anything
Without PostHog or Mixpanel you cannot see where people drop off, which QR placement drives scans, whether Bandra WF really is #1, or if the weekend spike is real. Every decision you make is a guess. The data in this dashboard is illustrative — not real.
→ Drop PostHog snippet into index.html (15-min task, free up to 1M events/month). We can do this now.
🟡 High — Act This Month
Weekends drive 3× more traffic — you're not capitalising on it
Saturday + Sunday account for ~60% of weekly visitors on just 2 of 7 days. Mumbai goes to brunch at Earth Cafe on weekends. You have no weekend-specific activation: no extra sampling, no staff briefing, no QR highlight. The ROI on weekend-only effort is very high.
→ Add a weekend staff script + double sample stock Sat–Sun. Test at Bandra WF first, then roll out.
🟡 High — Act This Month
Bandra Waterfield is the hero outlet — study and replicate it
35% of leads from one outlet means something is working there that isn't at the others — QR placement, staff awareness, customer profile, or table proximity to the caddy. You need to know which, because replicating it at Juhu and BKC alone could add 80+ leads/month.
→ Physically visit Bandra WF. Photograph QR placement. Brief staff at all 5 outlets using that standard.
🔵 Medium — This Quarter
The chip→form gap (60% → 34%) is your biggest conversion leak
People engage enough to select their outlet — they're genuinely curious — but only 56% of those proceed to touch the form. The offer headline "Ask our staff for your free pop" may feel passive on mobile. Test "Claim your free pop below" or moving the form above the pillars section.
→ A/B test CTA copy and form position. PostHog feature flags make this a 1-day build.
🟢 Opportunity
You're building one of the richest pet-owner databases in India
Name, breed, age, weight, WhatsApp, outlet — this is richer data than most pet brands collect at acquisition. Once Klaviyo is live, segment by breed + weight for hyper-personalised flows. Small dog owners are highly loyal and under-served. This list is worth far more than free pop giveaways.
→ Design Klaviyo flow: Welcome (Day 0) → Breed-specific education (Day 3) → First purchase offer (Day 7).
